ABOUT
Seishinkai
Our History

Seishinkai is a traditional karate organization that values personal growth and health over competition. Students from age 3 to 83 take part, and we have been active around Sapporo for more than 50 years. Children build healthy bodies through age-appropriate training, adults and seniors maintain their fitness, and women learn practical self-defense — with instruction tailored to each. We also run classes for students not attending school and welcome international members, providing an environment where anyone can train with confidence.

What We Teach

Supporting Children's Growth

Exercise matched to each stage of development builds fitness, focus, and courtesy.

Health & Fitness

Open to Everyone
