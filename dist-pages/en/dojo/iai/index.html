IAIDO

Seishinkai Iaido Division

[Seishinkai Iaido Division]
The Seishinkai Iaido Division got its start in 2005 (Heisei 17), led by Maekawa Shihan, Muroki Shihan, and the late Mimura Kayoko Shihan, at the Shiroishi Seishinkan Dojo.
Today we practice at the Seishinkan Headquarters Dojo. About half of our members are women.
We are currently welcoming new members.
Seniors aged 60 and over are especially welcome!!
Members currently range from junior high school students to 82 years old

A Lifelong Hobby

Sundays from 14:00
※Autumn/Winter/Spring hours
During the height of summer heat, sessions may begin in the evening instead

Opening times may be delayed due to traffic conditions, so please wait if this happens.
※For inquiries, please contact our office.
Trial classes are available for 1,000 yen.
We also offer one-time private seminars (tuition: 5,000–10,000 yen).
