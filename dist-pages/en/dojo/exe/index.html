PERSONAL TRAINING

Personal Training Karate Exercise

Individual lessons (including iaido and self-defense lessons)
[Seishinkai karate is a full-body conditioning exercise that avoids placing excessive strain on any one part of the body]
Seishinkai karate is not a competitive sport karate (modern karate), so it centers on the basics and kata.
It is an ideal conditioning exercise for middle-aged people looking to combat metabolic syndrome and for women who want to lose weight.
One member actually lost 20kg in a year. You can diet in a healthy way without straining your body.
We can also accommodate working people individually, so you can maintain a steady practice routine.
If you haven't had success at a sports club or with dietary therapy, please come and try this out.
Sessions are held by individual appointment (reservation required) outside of the headquarters dojo's regular practice days. As with regular classes, you can also earn kyu/dan rank.
