International Karate & Kobudo Exchange

[Foreign masters and karate instructors visit Seishinkai Karate from abroad]
Every year, foreign karate instructors from overseas visit the Seishinkai Headquarters Dojo.
Many of them come to Seishinkai in Hokkaido in search of karate as a traditional culture rather than a competitive sport, and then go on to Okinawa (and, conversely, some come to Sapporo, Hokkaido after first visiting Okinawa).
Many foreign masters, having come up through competitive karate, arrive at classical (koryu) karate as they seek out the roots of karate to make up for what training alone could not provide.

International Karate & Kobudo Exchange

Visitors have come from countries including Sweden, Italy, Pakistan, Egypt, Australia, France, Argentina, Sri Lanka, India, Kuwait, Ireland, and the United States.

Accommodation

We have a short term accommodation facility available for our international visitors.
