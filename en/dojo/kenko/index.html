HEALTH CLASS

Seishinkai Health Exercises

[Seishinkai Senior Karate Health Class / Health Taijutsu]
The Seishinkai Health Division began its activities in 2010 (Heisei 22), based on the Muroki-style health exercises (devised by the late Sensei Yoichi Muroki), incorporating cool-down exercise elements and breathing techniques from the taijutsu (karate kobudo) practiced at Seishinkai.
Afterward, we ran health exercise programs incorporating elements of seitai (bodywork) and various taijutsu techniques as one-off seminars, but we decided to hold regular classes with the aim of helping middle-aged and older adults lead healthy daily lives.
Excellent full-body exercise and cool-down movements improve circulation and boost physical strength (immunity).

We are currently accepting advance registration for the Senior Karate Health Class and the Health Taijutsu Class.
