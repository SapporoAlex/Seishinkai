INOUE DOJO

Seishinkai Karate-do Federation — Inoue Dojo

[Seishinkai Atsubetsu — Inoue Dojo]
The Kawashimo Karate Class began activities in April 2001 (Heisei 13) at the Genshirin Hall in Higashi-Atsubetsu.
After 10 years there, it moved its base to the current Higashi-Atsubetsu Children's Hall in August 2011 (Heisei 23).
The Kawakita Karate Class started in October 2009 (Heisei 21) as a class under direct headquarters control, and came under the management of Inoue-juku in October 2016 (Heisei 28).
In January 2023 (Reiwa 5), a dedicated dojo — the "Inoue Dojo" — was completed in Atsubetsu-Nishi, and the Kawakita and Higashi-Atsubetsu classes were merged into it.
If you can't make it to the Inoue Dojo, please consider the nearby Kawashimo Branch, Kita-Shiroishi Branch, Atsubetsu-Minami Branch, Momijidai Branch, or Shin-Sapporo Branch.
Inoue-juku places particular emphasis on building children's physical strength and mental resilience, and also serves as Seishinkai's full-contact kumite training class — it has produced Hokkaido-wide champions in both the Junior and General divisions.
